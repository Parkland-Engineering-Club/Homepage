Welcome to the Parkland Engineering Club Website

The Engineering Club at Parkland College provides students with opportunities to delve into topics in engineering and physics through hands-on projects, challenging questions, talks from industry professionals, and more. We meet every Wednesday at 2pm in room M126. It doesn't matter how much experience you have, what major you are in, or how much you hate physics, the Engineering Club is a great place to learn new things, meet some great people, and ocasionally grab a slice of pizza.
